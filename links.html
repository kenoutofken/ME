My favorite creative resources on the web!

This page features a curated list of websites and videos that have inspired me in my creative journey. Whether you’re looking for design inspiration or educational content to sharpen your skills, these resources are my go-to picks. Besides these links, on the right hand side of the page you will find a few links to some great videos for design related education. In my years of teaching design, I often showed videos from these creators to my students, which provided a simplified and visual way for them to learn often times complex design related topics. The videos are just a snippet of what these creators offer on their channel, so use some of the videos as a starting point to delve further if you are interested in the world of design! I hope you find them as useful and motivating as I have!

  1. Dribble.com: A vibrant community of designers showcasing their work. From UI/UX design to illustration, this platform is perfect for sparking new ideas.
  2. Behance.net: An online portfolio platform that features some of the best creative work across industries. A must-visit for design inspiration.
  3. Pinterest.com: My go-to for collecting ideas on design trends, typography, and color palettes. It’s a great way to explore different aesthetics.
  4. Designspiration.com: Designspiration is an online platform and creative community that serves as a source of visual inspiration for designers, artists, and creatives across various industries.
  5. Awwwards.com: Awwwards is a platform that recognizes and showcases the best web design and development work from around the world, celebrating innovation, creativity, and usability in digital design.
